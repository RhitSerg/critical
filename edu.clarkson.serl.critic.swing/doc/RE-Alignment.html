Confusion with Alignment

You are using an alignment-related API method in your code [Take Me There]. There are other related methods that provide other kinds of alignment functionalities as well and may confuse you. I thought you should know about their functionalities to make the right decision on choosing the correct one. Those methods are as follows:

  1. setAlignmentX() and setAlignmentY() of JComponent used with parameters such as JComponent.TOP_ALIGNMENT, JComponent.LEFT_ALIGNMENT, etc. These methods are designed to be used with BoxLayout to position the widget in each cell of BoxLayout.
  2. setHorizontalAlignment() and setVerticalAlignment() of JLabel and JButton used with parameters such as JComponent.TOP, JComponent.LEFT, etc. These methods are used by a component to align its content within itself irrespective of the layout manager used.
  3. setHorizontalTextPosition() and setVerticalTextPosition() of JLabel and JButton used with the same parameters as 2. These methods are used by a component to align the text within a JLabel or a JButton relative to its icon image. You can browse to a short tutorial on text positioning from here.
